Rafting Site Plan

Ehis Jacob

Overview

Purpose

With the belief that bringing people together in the outdoors and inspiring people to push their limits is the soul of rafting, Ehis Jacob created his Rafting company "Reme Craft" to help drive the rafting community into the future. From expedition boating to helping new people coming into the rafting world, Ehis puts his passion to work developing initiatives focused on wonder of rivers throughout the world.

Audience

Reme's Craft target market is characteristically made up of recreational travelers with active lifestyles. This eliminates the greater portion of the travel industry, allowing Reme Craft to focus more closely on its market demographics. The only acceptted customers of Reme Craft should be business class people, family, college students, etc. Age range: 25-60. Gender: 80% male, 20% female.
